About Me

The endeavour to learn about UAVs began four years ago. Aerial Robotics offered the enthusiasm for drone racing and pursuing projects. The result was building, PID tuning and flight data analysis of five multirotors, missions on autonomous waypoint navigation, a UAV fleet based on Swarm Technology for search-rescue missions was developed, securing the second place in abstract submission round. Current work (prof. Hemendra Arya, MAVLab)-achieving a robust multirotor control against strong winds, inspiring the study of controllers and wind modelling. Research interests- Robust Control, Hardware-Mechanical design, Motion Planning, Autonomous hybrid UAVs.
